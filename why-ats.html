Career Path

Stability

There’s no need to worry whether or not you’ll receive the paycheck you earned. Since 1955, ATS has been a staple in the trucking industry, continuing to grow as the years go on. It’s been said before, we’re not going anywhere but down the road. When you decide to make ATS your career home, hopefully you can say the same.

Family Matters

When we say family matters at ATS, we really mean it. That goes for your loved ones and your work family. That means you’ll be treated like a member of the family when you’re at work and you’ll get home to see your real family when you need to. Maybe it’s the fact that ATS is still family-owned and three generations strong, or maybe it’s the fact that treating our Drivers and Employees with respect is just the right thing to do.

Drive Quality Matters

What does drive quality mean? A lot of things, really. ATS Drivers use the highest quality tractors and trailers in the business while hauling quality freight for quality customers. Ultimately, it means less downtime and more money in your pocket.

Variety Matters

It’s hard to get bored with the freight variety ATS has to offer. The asset sitting on or in your trailer will change as much as the route you take to safely deliver it. That also means there are always new securement methods to ensure it arrives in proper condition. Looking to stay sharp with new challenges? Look no further.

Safety

ATS is Safety Driven. That’s the motto we live by. From the well-maintained equipment to the tie-down procedures and the way you’re treated by office personnel, you’ll learn safety truly does matter at ATS. Safety is much more than a department at ATS, it trickles throughout the entire company. Safe travels!
